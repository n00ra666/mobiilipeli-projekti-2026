# Challenge
## Basic game idea
Endless runner, try to get as far as possible. The speed at which the player moves gets **progressively faster**, requiring quick reaction times and decision making. The player starts with 2 lives. If the player hits *an enemy*, *an obstacle*, *a platform edge*, or *falls off the map*, they lose a life. After losing all lives, the run is over.

## Obstacles
**Obstacles** are stationary objects that the player needs to either *jump over* or *dash through*. Dashing through an obstacle rewards the player with points.
## Enemies
**Enemies** are moving objects that the player must avoid colliding with as *they can't be dashed through*. *Jumping over* an enemy rewards the player with points. The enemies follow a set path on the platform and their movement speed increases in sync with the player's speed.
## Platforms
**Platforms** are spawned at three different *levels*: top, middle, and bottom. The player can switch between the levels by following a path higher or falling down from a higher level. As the player's movement speed increases, they have to time their jumps and dashes more accurately to avoid hitting the edge of oncoming platforms.
